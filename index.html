About Me

Welcome to my portfolio! I'm a Computer Engineering student and passionate software developer with a comprehensive background in both software development and IT infrastructure. My journey combines hands-on technical experience with a strong academic foundation in computer engineering.

As a Computer Engineering student, I've developed expertise in full-stack development, working with JavaScript, TypeScript, Python, and various modern frameworks to build efficient, scalable solutions. Beyond software development, I have extensive experience in IT and hardware support, troubleshooting complex technical issues, and maintaining robust IT infrastructure systems.

I'm particularly interested in cybersecurity and IT support architecture, focusing on designing secure, resilient systems that protect data and ensure reliable operations. This interest drives me to stay current with the latest security practices and architectural patterns in enterprise IT environments.

During my university experience, I've been actively involved in leadership and academic enrichment. I was a member of FEAST (Freshman Exemplifying Aggie Spirit Together), a freshman leadership organization that provided me with opportunities to develop leadership skills and connect with fellow undergraduate students. Additionally, I had the incredible opportunity to study abroad in Belgium, where I completed coursework in Computer Architecture and Advanced Computer Architecture, gaining international perspective and deepening my understanding of low-level system design.

I'm always eager to learn new technologies and take on challenging projects that push the boundaries of what's possible, whether it's building modern web applications, designing secure IT architectures, or solving complex hardware and software integration challenges.
